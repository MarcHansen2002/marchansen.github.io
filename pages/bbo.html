Bullet Blast-Off is a project I worked on over the summer break in either 2021 or 2022 (I'm not 100% sure which). I used unreal engine to create the project and learnt about many things in game development from this project such as replication and networking to allow for multiplayer connections between players. I was wanting to make this into a full game but making my own 3D models and levels made me lose a lot of motivation for the project, along with university starting back out I quickly ran out of time to work on the project, however I still enjoyed the process and learnt things from the experience of creating Bullet Blast-Off
