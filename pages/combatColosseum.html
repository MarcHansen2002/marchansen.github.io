Combat Colosseum - Unity, C#, Group Work

Project Download      





Description of screenshot



Combat Colosseum was made in Unity using C# in a group of 2 programmers, 2 artists and 3 designers for a university assignment (hence the repository not being available). In the project I was responsible for creating the systems for combat, leveling & upgrades and the AI of enemies. The project was initially just for a the assignment, however 3 of us decided to continue for a few weeks after submission to polish the game more.
